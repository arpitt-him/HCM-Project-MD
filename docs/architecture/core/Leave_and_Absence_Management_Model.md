## Purpose

Defines structures and lifecycle management for employee leaves and absences, including paid and unpaid leave types, eligibility, duration tracking, and payroll impact behaviour.

This model governs leave-driven time suppression, substitution, and payroll result behaviour.

Leave events influence:

- worked time interpretation
- payroll result generation
- accrual and entitlement balances
- compliance validation
- payroll recalculation workflows

Leave outcomes must remain deterministic, traceable, and replay-safe.

---

## 1. Leave Scope

Supported leave types: PTO, Vacation, Sick Leave, Holiday Leave, Personal Leave, LOA, FMLA, Short-Term Disability, Long-Term Disability, Military Leave, Jury Duty Leave.

## 2. Core Leave_Request Entity

Leave_Request_ID, Employee_ID, Employment_ID, Leave_Type, Request_Date, Leave_Start_Date, Leave_End_Date, Leave_Status, Leave_Reason_Code.

Additional leave attributes may include:

Leave_Request_Version_ID  
Original_Leave_Request_ID  
Approval_Reference_ID  
Eligibility_Profile_ID  
Payroll_Impact_Profile_ID  

These attributes support replay-safe recalculation and correction workflows.

## 3. Leave_Type Definition

Leave_Type_ID, Leave_Type_Name, Paid_or_Unpaid_Flag, Accrual_Eligible_Flag, Maximum_Duration, Carryover_Allowed_Flag.

Additional leave-type attributes may include:

Payroll_Substitution_Code  
Payroll_Suppression_Flag  
Premium_Eligibility_Override  
Jurisdiction_ID  

These attributes define how leave modifies payroll computation outcomes.

## 4. Leave Status Lifecycle

Requested, Approved, Denied, Scheduled, Active, Completed, Cancelled.

Only Approved or Active leave states shall be eligible for payroll consumption.

Cancelled or Denied leave requests must not influence payroll computation.

Completed leave records remain immutable except through governed correction workflows.

## 5. Leave Duration Tracking

Total_Requested_Days, Total_Approved_Days, Leave_Balance_Impact, Remaining_Balance. Leave balances must update in alignment with accrual models.

## 6. Payroll Impact Handling

Leave events modify payroll behaviour through time suppression or substitution logic.

Examples:

Paid leave → generates substitution earnings result lines  
Unpaid leave → suppresses scheduled earnings  
Disability leave → generates governed disability earnings logic  

Leave-triggered payroll behaviour shall:

- generate structured result lines
- remain traceable to Leave_Request_ID
- preserve linkage to affected Time_Entry_ID values
- integrate with Employee_Payroll_Result processing

Leave-derived payroll results must remain deterministic across replay and correction workflows.

## 7. Leave Accrual Integration

Accrual_Rate, Accrual_Frequency, Maximum_Accrual_Limit, Carryover_Rules. Accrual behaviour integrates with accumulator models.

Accrual impacts generated by leave activity shall be processed through governed accumulator mutation workflows.

Leave balance changes must remain:

- versioned
- traceable
- replay-safe

Accumulator impacts must reference originating Leave_Request_ID values.

## 8. Compliance Leave Handling

FMLA leave tracking, military leave protections, state-specific leave requirements. Compliance rules must be configurable by jurisdiction.

Compliance leave eligibility shall be resolved through rule-driven evaluation using jurisdiction-specific policy definitions.

Compliance leave records must support regulatory audit reconstruction.

## 9. Return to Work Handling

Return_Date, Work_Status_Restored, Benefit_Reactivation_Flag, Payroll_Reactivation_Flag.

Return-to-work events may trigger:

- payroll reactivation
- benefit reinstatement
- schedule restoration
- accrual resumption

Return events must remain linked to originating leave records.

## 10. Historical Tracking and Audit

All leave activity must be historically preserved, including:

- request creation
- approval decisions
- schedule adjustments
- duration modifications
- balance impacts
- payroll result effects

Historical leave records must support:

- payroll replay
- compliance audit
- correction workflows

Leave history shall never be silently overwritten.
